About Us

Sentinel Capital & Compliance is a U.S.-based compliance-driven private equity and consultancy firm focused on strategic equity acquisition in distressed international banks. Our mission is to serve as the trusted intermediary between financial institutions and U.S. regulatory bodies such as OFAC.

Through long-term partnerships, regulatory rehabilitation, and FATF-aligned structural reform, we restore legitimacy, transparency, and USD access to institutions critical to economic stability in the Middle East and beyond.
